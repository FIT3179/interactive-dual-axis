Are cigarette sales and death rate correlated?

In this activity, you will play with dual-axis line charts to see how the type of mapping, scale, and offset of the axes can affect your perception of the data.

The blue line — is average cigarette sales per adult per day.

The orange line — is average cancer death rate per 100k male.
